Master Mind

MASTERMIND WITH NUMBERS

How do you play?

Player one composes a secret 4-digit number which his/her opponent must figure out within ten tries. Player one guesses the digits and player two responds with hints using plus (+) and minus (-) to let player one know how accurate the guess was. A plus(+) indicates that one digit is correct, minus(-) indicates that one digit is correct but in a wrong place.
